Sharing lets another simpleBudget user view and edit your budget. Great for couples, roommates, or anyone managing money together.

## How to share

1. Open the **Settings** page (gear icon in the bottom nav)
2. Tap **Share Budget**
3. Scan you QR code of the person you want to share with (they must already have a simpleBudget account)
- to find this QR code, they need to go to **Settings** and tap **Show My QR Code**
- if you can't scan their QR Code, have them send you their user id (found on the QR Code page) and you can enter it manually.
4. Tap **Share**

The other person will see your budget appear in their budget selector next time they open the app.

## What shared users can do

- View all sections, categories, and transactions
- Add and edit transactions
- Add and edit categories and sections
- Change budgeted amounts

## What shared users cannot do

- Delete the budget
- Remove other shared users
- Change the budget name

Only the budget creator (owner) can do these things.

## Removing access

To stop sharing with someone:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Share Budget**
2. You'll see a list of people the budget is shared with
3. Tap the remove button next to their name

They'll lose access immediately, but any transactions they added will remain.

## Tips

- **Each person should install the PWA** on their own device for the best experience
- **You'll both see changes in real time** — no need to refresh or sync manually
- **Consider naming your budget clearly** (e.g., "Household Budget") so it's obvious in the budget selector
