Getting started with simpleBudget takes about 2 minutes. Here's the full walkthrough.

## 1. Create your account

1. Go to [app.simplebudget.com/signup](https://app.simplebudget.com/signup)
2. Enter your name, email, and a password (8+ characters)
3. Tap **Sign Up**
4. Check your email and click the verification link

Once verified, you can log in.

## 2. Create your first budget

After your first login, you'll be prompted to create a budget:

1. Give it a name (e.g., "My Budget" or "Household Budget")
2. Check **"Include default sections & categories"** if you want a head start — this creates common sections like Income, Housing, Food, Transportation, etc.
3. Tap **Add Budget**

If you skip the template, you'll start with a blank budget and can add sections manually.

## 3. Set your amounts

Now you're on the Budget page for the current month:

1. You'll see your sections listed (Income at the top, expenses below)
2. Tap any category to set its budgeted amount
3. Start with income — enter your expected take-home pay
4. Then work through expenses — rent, groceries, subscriptions, etc.

The summary at the top shows your planned income vs. planned expenses. Aim for the difference to be $0 or positive.

## 4. Start tracking

As you spend money throughout the month:

1. Tap the **+** button (bottom of screen on mobile, or "Add Transaction" on desktop)
2. Enter the amount, pick a category, and add a short description
3. The budget page updates in real time to show how much you have left in each category

## Tips for your first month

- **Don't stress about getting amounts perfect.** You'll adjust as you learn your actual spending patterns.
- **Check in weekly.** A quick 2-minute review keeps you on track without feeling like a chore.
- **Use the "Copy budget outline" feature** next month to carry your structure forward without re-entering everything.
