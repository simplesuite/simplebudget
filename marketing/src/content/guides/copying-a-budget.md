At the start of a new month, you don't need to rebuild your budget from scratch. The **Copy budget outline** feature duplicates your sections and categories (with their amounts) into a new month.

## How to copy

1. Navigate to the **month you want to copy FROM** (e.g., if January was good, go to January)
2. Tap the **three-dot menu** (⋮) in the top-left of the Budget page
3. Tap **"Copy budget outline"**
4. Select the target month and year
5. Confirm

This copies:
- All sections (names and types)
- All categories (names and budgeted amounts)

This does **not** copy:
- Transactions (you start fresh each month)

## When to use it

- **Every month** — most people copy last month's budget and then tweak a few amounts
- **Seasonal adjustments** — copy a month that matches your expected spending pattern (e.g., copy last December for this December)

## Tips

- **Adjust after copying.** Your income or expenses might change month to month — review the amounts after copying.
- **Navigate to the new month** after copying to verify everything looks right.
- **You can copy to any future month** — useful if you want to plan a few months ahead.
